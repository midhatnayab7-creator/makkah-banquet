Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 15-30 days in advance, especially during wedding season (November-February). However, we try our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

Can I customize the menu?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable menus. You can mix and match items from different packages or request special dishes. Menu tasting can be arranged.

What is the guest capacity?

Our halls can accommodate from 100 to 500+ guests. We have multiple halls to suit events of all sizes - from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space for guests. Valet parking is also available with our Gold and Platinum packages.
